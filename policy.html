Privacy Policy for Logistics Engineer Pro

Last updated: March 2026

Logistics Engineer Pro ("the App") is a mobile application developed by Tatarinov Vladimir, designed for use in logistics operations, warehouse management, and internal productivity. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles data.

1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information of the user. All data is stored locally on the user's device and is never sent to external servers.

2. Device Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions for local functionality only:

  • Camera — used exclusively for scanning barcodes within the App. No images or video are stored or transmitted.
  • Microphone — used exclusively for voice input in the AI Assistant feature. No audio is stored or transmitted.

These permissions are used solely to provide App functionality and do not involve any data collection or transfer to third parties.

3. Local Device Storage Only

All operational data (documents, warehouse locations, inventory results, calculations) is stored only on your device using local storage.

4. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use external servers, analytics, cloud storage, advertising systems, or tracking technologies.

5. No Account, Login, or Registration

The App does not require any account creation or login.

6. Data Control

All data inside the App is controlled entirely by the user. You may delete the App at any time — all stored data will be permanently removed from the device.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, but it also does not collect any information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if required by app stores or applicable law. Changes will be reflected on this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, please contact:

Developer: Tatarinov Vladimir

Email: v.tatarinov75@gmail.com

物流工程师专业版 隐私政策

最后更新：2026年3月

物流工程师专业版（以下简称"本应用"）由开发者 Tatarinov Vladimir 开发， 专为物流运营、仓库管理及内部生产力提升而设计。 本隐私政策说明本应用如何处理数据。

1. 数据收集

本应用不收集、存储、传输或共享用户的任何个人信息。 所有数据仅存储在用户设备本地，从不发送至外部服务器。

2. 设备权限

本应用申请以下设备权限，仅用于本地功能：

  • 摄像头 — 仅用于应用内扫描条形码。不存储或传输任何图像或视频。
  • 麦克风 — 仅用于AI助手功能中的语音输入。不存储或传输任何音频。

上述权限仅用于提供应用功能，不涉及任何数据收集或向第三方传输。

3. 仅限设备本地存储

所有运营数据（文档、仓库位置、库存结果、计算结果） 仅使用本地存储保存在您的设备上。

4. 无第三方服务

本应用不使用外部服务器、分析系统、云存储、广告系统或追踪技术。

5. 无账户、登录或注册

本应用无需创建账户或登录。

6. 数据控制

应用内的所有数据完全由用户控制。 您可以随时删除本应用——设备上存储的所有数据将被永久删除。

7. 儿童隐私

本应用不面向13岁以下儿童，且不收集任何信息。

8. 政策变更

如应用商店或适用法律有要求，我们可能会更新本隐私政策。 变更将在本页面上体现。
