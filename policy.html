Privacy Policy for Logistics Engineer Pro

Last updated: December 2025

Logistics Engineer Pro (“the App”) is a mobile application designed for personal use in logistics operations, warehouse management, and internal productivity. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles data.

1. No Personal Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information of the user. All data is stored locally on the user’s device.

2. Local Device Storage Only

All operational data (documents, items, warehouse locations, inventory results) is stored only on your device using local storage.

3. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use external servers, analytics, cloud storage, advertising systems, or tracking technologies.

4. No Account, Login, or Registration

The App does not require any account creation or login.

5. Data Control

All data inside the App is controlled entirely by the user. You may delete the App at any time — all stored data will be permanently removed from the device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, but it also does not collect any information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if required by the App Store or Google Play. Changes will be reflected on this page.
